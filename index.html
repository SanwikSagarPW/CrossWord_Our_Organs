HOW TO PLAY

1 Click on a clue from the ACROSS or DOWN list to highlight the corresponding word in the grid.
2 Type your answer directly into the highlighted cells. The cursor will automatically move to the next cell.
3 Use arrow keys to navigate between cells or click any cell to start typing there.
4 Click "Check Completion" to see if all cells are filled. The Submit button will appear when ready.
5 Click "Submit Puzzle" to check your answers. Correct answers stay locked, incorrect ones flash and can be corrected.
